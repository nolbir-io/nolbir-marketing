## Issue Severity

- **Critical:** Blocks indexing or severely harms discoverability.
- **High:** Likely to impact rankings or conversion.
- **Medium:** Optimization opportunity.
- **Low:** Hygiene improvement.

## Cadence

- Run full audit monthly.
- Re-check critical/high items weekly until resolved.
