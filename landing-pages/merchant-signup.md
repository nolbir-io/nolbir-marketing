**Page purpose:** Convert paid + organic traffic from Uzbekistan merchants — including established brands selling on Uzum, Yandex Market, and Ozon — into a booked call ("Launch plan call").
**Primary CTA:** Book a 30-min launch plan call
**Secondary CTA:** See what's included (anchor link to capabilities section)
**Audience:**
- **Primary:** Established Uzbekistan merchants currently selling primarily through marketplaces (Uzum, Yandex Market, Ozon) who want to own their commerce, lower commission costs, and build a direct channel.
- **Secondary:** Mid-market and enterprise merchants modernizing their commerce stack — CTOs, heads of e-commerce, ops/operations leads.

**Traffic assumption:** Cold + warm. Many visitors are running real revenue today (mostly through marketplaces) and are evaluating whether to invest in their own commerce infrastructure.

Marketplaces are easy to get started, hard to justify profit margins as you scale. They don't run your business.

Now the hard questions show up:

- **Commission costs scale faster than margin.** Every additional order is another commission payment, not another point of leverage. The math gets worse as you grow, not better.
- **Scale** - become ready for scale from day one. Sell, Manage, Grow without IT overhead.
- **You don't own the customer.** The marketplace owns the relationship, the data, and the next purchase. You're acquiring buyers you'll never get to keep.

And if you also try to run your own store on the side, you hit the second problem: stitching together payments, delivery, BNPL, fiscalization, compliance, language, and a Telegram channel into something that actually works. Most teams give up after the third integration meeting.
