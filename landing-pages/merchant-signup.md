# Landing page — Merchant signup (English source draft)

**Page purpose:** Convert paid + organic traffic from Uzbekistan merchants — including established brands selling on Uzum, Yandex Market, and Ozon — into a booked call ("Launch plan call").
**Primary CTA:** Book a 30-min launch plan call
**Secondary CTA:** See what's included (anchor link to capabilities section)
**Audience:**
- **Primary:** Established Uzbekistan merchants currently selling primarily through marketplaces (Uzum, Yandex Market, Ozon) who want to own their commerce, lower commission costs, and build a direct channel.
- **Secondary:** Mid-market and enterprise merchants modernizing their commerce stack — CTOs, heads of e-commerce, ops/operations leads.
- **Tertiary:** Digital-first SMBs with clear near-term intent to launch online.
**Traffic assumption:** Cold + warm. Many visitors are running real revenue today (mostly through marketplaces) and are evaluating whether to invest in their own commerce infrastructure.
**Positioning:** Own is the **operating system for Uzbekistan e-commerce** — not a marketplace replacement. We add direct channels (custom storefront, Telegram) to your marketplace presence, then unify orders, inventory, customers, payments, and compliance behind one platform.
**Source-of-truth context:** `.agents/product-marketing-context.md`, `competitor-profiles/_summary.md`
**Translation:** Uzbek primary, Russian secondary per `brand-voice/language-matrix.md`. Mirror headline + CTAs first.

## 7. How it works

**Section header:**
How onboarding actually goes.

**Lede:**
No long discovery. No surprises in week six. From first call to first transactions across channels:

**4 numbered steps:**

1. **Book a launch plan call (30 min).**
   We learn what you sell, where you sell today, and what's in your way. You leave with a written launch plan — even if you don't move forward with us.

2. **Agree scope and channel sequence.**
   Storefront, marketplaces, payments, logistics, language, and content — mapped into stages with owners and dates. You see exactly what "live" means for each channel, and the order we launch them in.

3. **Go live, together.**
   We onboard alongside your team. No checklist hand-off. Direct channels first. Marketplaces per the roadmap.

4. **Reach transacting. Then run it yourself.**
   We watch the first orders, fix what blocks conversion or sync, and hand you a system your team can operate without us in the room.

**Why this section:** Mirrors the activation arc in section 5 but from the merchant's point of view. Updated to make sequencing explicit (direct channels first, marketplaces per roadmap) so prospects don't expect day-1 Uzum sync if it isn't ready yet.
