# Funnel Definition

Unified funnel stages and operational definitions.

## Stages

1. **Visitor:** Unique person reaching owned properties.
2. **Lead:** Contact captured with valid business context.
3. **Qualified lead:** Matches ICP and has near-term intent.
4. **Sales-qualified lead:** Confirmed fit after conversation.
5. **Signed merchant:** Commercial agreement accepted.
6. **Live merchant:** Technical onboarding complete.
7. **Transacting merchant:** First successful commercial transaction.

## Stage Exit Criteria

- Visitor -> Lead: Form completion or direct inbound with contact data.
- Lead -> Qualified: Meets ICP + explicit problem/need.
- Qualified -> SQL: Discovery confirms readiness and budget/path.
- SQL -> Signed: Contract accepted.
- Signed -> Live: Integration/onboarding complete and test passes.
- Live -> Transacting: First qualifying transaction received.

## Core Conversion Metrics

- Visitor -> Lead conversion
- Lead -> Qualified conversion
- Qualified -> SQL conversion
- SQL -> Signed win rate
- Signed -> Live activation rate
- Live -> Transacting rate

## SLA Targets (Initial)

- Lead qualification within 2 business days.
- Signed to live onboarding within 14 days.
- Weekly funnel inspection in Growth Ops.
