# KPI Tree

North-star and driver metrics for the 6-month foundation horizon.

## North-Star Candidate

- **North-star metric:** Active transacting merchants (to finalize in open questions register).

## KPI Tree

- **North-star:** Active transacting merchants
  - **Activation volume**
    - Merchants signed
    - Merchants live
    - Merchants transacting in 30 days
  - **Acquisition efficiency**
    - Qualified leads per channel
    - Cost per qualified lead
    - CAC by channel
  - **Revenue quality proxy**
    - Repeat transaction rate (30-day)
    - Early retention of activated merchants

## Leading Indicators (Weekly)

- New qualified leads
- Sales/demo meetings booked
- Offer acceptance rate
- Time-to-activation from signed to live
- Landing page to lead conversion rate

## Ownership

- KPI definitions: Analytics Owner
- Target setting: Marketing Lead + Founder/CEO
- Weekly reporting: Analytics Owner
