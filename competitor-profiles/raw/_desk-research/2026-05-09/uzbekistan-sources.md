## Caveats

- Numbers across sources differ by year and methodology (KPMG/ECDB/Statista). Treat any single figure as a **range**, not a point estimate.
- Some sources are press releases or vendor self-reports (Uzum, Robosell, Magicstore, Delever) — useful for **positioning** but not for independent market sizing.
- Regulatory context is evolving fast (2024–2025); always check the latest before publishing claims externally.
