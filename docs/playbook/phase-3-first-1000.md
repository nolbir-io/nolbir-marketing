# Phase 3 — First 1,000 (Scaled Acquisition)

> Industrialize a channel mix that delivers predictable merchant acquisition.

## Goal

900 more merchants on top of Phase 2, through channels that operate on a budget-and-targets cadence rather than personal hustle. By the end, Mamurify has a marketing organization, not a founder with a Telegram account.

## Motion

Channel-led. A marketing function owns demand. A sales function owns conversion. The founder's role shifts to category-creation work — talks, partnerships, narrative — not individual deals.

## Marketing artifacts to produce

- Quarterly campaign briefs per channel
- Localized site & content in the priority language matrix (see [`../../brand-voice/`](../../brand-voice/))
- Refreshed positioning if competitive landscape has shifted
- Public metrics / milestone moments (the "we hit 500", "we hit 1,000" beats)
- Mature performance reporting — weekly, monthly, quarterly cadence

## Exit criteria

This phase ends when 1,000 merchants are live. After that, the playbook either restarts at a new milestone (10,000) or pivots to a different shape (geographic expansion, new product line, etc.).
