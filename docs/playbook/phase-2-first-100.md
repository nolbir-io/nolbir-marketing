# Phase 2 — First 100 (Early Adopters)

> Prove the motion repeats without the founder in every conversation.

## Goal

90 more merchants on top of the Phase 1 design partners, acquired through a small set of repeatable channels rather than personal outreach. The point isn't volume — it's *evidence that anyone other than the founder can close*.

## Motion

Hybrid. A first marketer/AE owns most conversations; the founder takes only the strategic accounts and the public-facing storytelling. Inbound starts to matter for the first time — even if it's still a small share.

## Marketing artifacts to produce

- 3–5 case studies from Phase 1 design partners
- Sales deck v1 — used by anyone, not just the founder
- Battlecards for the top 3 competitors (see [`../../competitive-brief/`](../../competitive-brief/))
- Live performance dashboard v1 (see [`../../performance-analytics/`](../../performance-analytics/))
- Editorial calendar with weekly publishing rhythm

## Exit criteria

Move to Phase 3 when:

- ≥ 100 merchants live.
- ≥ 50% of new merchants in the last 30 days closed without the founder in the room.
- We have ≥ 2 channels with predictable, measurable acquisition cost.
- Activation rate (signed → live → transacting) is known and stable.
