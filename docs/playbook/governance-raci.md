# Governance and RACI

Decision rights and accountability model for marketing execution.

## Core Roles

- **Founder/CEO:** Final strategic and budget approval.
- **Marketing Lead:** Owns GTM execution and cross-functional alignment.
- **Campaign Owner:** Executes campaign plans and channel delivery.
- **Content Lead:** Produces assets and editorial cadence.
- **Brand Lead:** Maintains voice standards and review quality.
- **Analytics Owner:** Owns KPI definitions, reporting, and experiment evaluation.
- **Localization Reviewer:** Approves UZ/RU language quality.

## Decision Rights

- **Playbook phase updates:** Founder/CEO decides, Marketing Lead recommends.
- **Budget allocation by channel:** Founder/CEO decides, Marketing Lead recommends.
- **Campaign launch go/no-go:** Marketing Lead decides.
- **Brand claim approval:** Brand Lead decides; Founder consulted for strategic claims.
- **KPI definition changes:** Analytics Owner recommends, Marketing Lead decides.
- **Localization final sign-off:** Localization Reviewer decides.

## RACI Matrix

| Workstream | R | A | C | I |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 6-month roadmap updates | Marketing Lead | Founder/CEO | Analytics Owner, Campaign Owner | All GTM members |
| Campaign brief creation | Campaign Owner | Marketing Lead | Content Lead, Analytics Owner | Founder/CEO |
| Content production | Content Lead | Marketing Lead | Brand Lead, Campaign Owner | Analytics Owner |
| Brand review and sign-off | Brand Lead | Marketing Lead | Localization Reviewer | Campaign Owner |
| KPI tree and funnel definitions | Analytics Owner | Marketing Lead | Founder/CEO | Campaign and Content owners |
| Experiment prioritization | Analytics Owner | Marketing Lead | Campaign Owner, Product/Activation | Founder/CEO |
| Monthly KPI review | Analytics Owner | Marketing Lead | Founder/CEO | All GTM members |

## Escalation Protocol

1. Try to resolve within workstream owner + accountable owner in 24 hours.
2. If unresolved, escalate to Marketing Lead in the next Growth Ops meeting.
3. If strategic trade-off remains, Founder/CEO makes final decision within 72 hours.

## Documentation Standard

- Any material decision must be logged in `docs/playbook/open-questions-register.md`.
- Every recurring artifact must have one DRI and last-updated timestamp.
