# Operating Cadence

Operating rhythm for NOLBIR marketing over the next 6 months.

## Weekly Growth Ops (60 min)

- **Owner:** Marketing lead
- **Participants:** Campaign owner, content owner, analytics owner, sales/activation representative
- **Inputs:** `performance-analytics/weekly-report-template.md`, campaign calendar, blocker list
- **Agenda:**
  1. Review last week KPI deltas and top/underperforming channels.
  2. Confirm this week launches and dependencies.
  3. Resolve blockers and assign owners with due dates.
- **Outputs:** Weekly commitments, risk updates, changes to experiment priorities.

## Weekly Brand and Content Review (45 min)

- **Owner:** Brand lead
- **Participants:** Content lead, language reviewer (UZ/RU), campaign owner
- **Inputs:** Draft queue from `content-creation/`, rubric from `brand-review/review-rubric.md`
- **Agenda:**
  1. Review highest-impact in-flight assets.
  2. Approve, request changes, or reject with clear rationale.
  3. Track systemic issues to update voice/language rules.
- **Outputs:** Approved ship list, feedback log entries, rule updates if needed.

## Biweekly Experiment Review (45 min)

- **Owner:** Growth/analytics owner
- **Participants:** Marketing lead, campaign owner, product/activation representative
- **Inputs:** `performance-analytics/experiments-log.md`
- **Agenda:**
  1. Evaluate active experiments against success criteria.
  2. Decide one action per test: scale, iterate, or stop.
  3. Prioritize next experiment backlog.
- **Outputs:** Decision per experiment and updated priorities.

## Monthly Business Review (90 min)

- **Owner:** Marketing lead
- **Participants:** Founders, marketing core team, sales/activation lead
- **Inputs:** Monthly KPI rollup, spend summary, campaign outcomes
- **Agenda:**
  1. Compare KPI performance against month target in `docs/plan-6m/`.
  2. Review CAC, payback assumptions, and channel mix.
  3. Confirm what to double down on next month.
- **Outputs:** Month plan adjustments and explicit leadership decisions.

## Quarterly GTM Checkpoint (Half-day)

- **Owner:** Founding team
- **Participants:** Cross-functional GTM leaders
- **Inputs:** Playbook phases and prior quarter performance
- **Agenda:**
  1. Reassess ICP and offer assumptions.
  2. Confirm phase exit criteria realism.
  3. Rebalance budget and team capacity.
- **Outputs:** Updated phase priorities and strategic change log.

## Meeting Hygiene Rules

- Every recurring meeting has a single owner and pre-read posted 24h before.
- Every decision records owner, date, and rationale in `docs/playbook/open-questions-register.md`.
- Unowned actions are invalid and must be assigned before meeting close.
