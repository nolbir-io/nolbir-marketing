# 6-Month Marketing Roadmap

Execution plan for building a stable marketing operating system.

## Planning Metadata

- **Plan owner:** Marketing Lead
- **Time horizon:** 6 months
- **Review cadence:** Monthly
- **Primary objective:** Build repeatable marketing foundations before aggressive scale.

## Month Sequence

- [Month 01](month-01.md) - Align ownership, definitions, and baseline metrics.
- [Month 02](month-02.md) - Launch first structured campaigns and measurement.
- [Month 03](month-03.md) - Improve quality, messaging consistency, and funnel conversion.
- [Month 04](month-04.md) - Scale what works and stop what does not.
- [Month 05](month-05.md) - Systematize partner, content, and SEO loops.
- [Month 06](month-06.md) - Consolidate learnings and prepare next planning cycle.

## Guardrails

- Do not add new channels without owner, hypothesis, and budget envelope.
- No campaign ships without review against brand rubric and KPI instrumentation.
- Every month closes with documented decisions and updates to the playbook.
