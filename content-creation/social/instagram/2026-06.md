**Audience:** Established Uzbekistan merchants selling on Uzum, Yandex Market, Ozon. Secondary: digital-first SMBs launching online.
**Goal:** Create interest, build authority, drive saves/DMs and traffic to the launch-plan call.
**Format mix:** 4 carousels, 2 reels. 6 posts — each earns its slot.
**Cadence:** Mon/Wed/Fri. 6 posts spanning 2026-06-08 → 2026-06-29.
**Campaign:** Phase 1 — First 10 (`campaigns/phase-1-brief.md`)
**Hashtag stub:** #Own #UzbekistanEcommerce #Tijorat #Marketplace #Uzum #YandexMarket #Ozon

**Voice rules:** Practical over hype. Clear over clever. No fabricated stats or testimonials — every numeric claim is either a structural fact or marked `[PROOF — to collect]`.

The visual stays the same across all three; only the text layer changes. Native review still required before publish per `brand-voice/language-matrix.md`.
