# Instagram — June 2026 batch

**Audience:** Established Uzbekistan merchants selling on Uzum, Yandex Market, Ozon. Secondary: digital-first SMBs launching online.
**Goal:** Create interest, build authority, drive saves/DMs and traffic to the launch-plan call.
**Format mix:** 4 carousels, 4 single-image, 2 reels, 2 quote/text cards.
**Cadence:** 3×/week (Mon/Wed/Fri). 12 posts spanning 2026-06-08 → 2026-07-03.
**Campaign:** Phase 1 — First 10 (`campaigns/phase-1-brief.md`)
**Hashtag stub:** #Own #OwnItDontRentIt #UzbekistanEcommerce #Tijorat #Marketplace #Uzum #YandexMarket #Ozon

**Voice rules:** Practical over hype. Clear over clever. No fabricated stats or testimonials — every numeric claim is either a structural fact or marked `[PROOF — to collect]`.

**Languages:** All drafts in English. Translate Uzbek (primary) + Russian (secondary) before publish per `brand-voice/language-matrix.md`.

**Campaign thesis:** marketplaces are useful distribution, but a merchant should not rent the storefront, customer relationship, data, and economics that determine whether the business compounds.

**CTA ladder:**
1. Save/share for high-friction education posts.
2. DM/comment keyword for practical assets: `PLAN`, `MARGIN`, `LIVE`, `TELEGRAM`.
3. Link in bio for the launch-plan call once the viewer understands the pain.

**Story support on publish days:** 2-3 frames per post:
- Frame 1: restate the hook as a poll or question.
- Frame 2: show one proof/detail from the post.
- Frame 3: repeat the keyword CTA or link sticker.

**Success signals:** saves, shares, profile visits, keyword DMs, link-in-bio clicks by post ID, and qualified launch-plan conversations.

## Production notes

- **Visuals:** produce from real product screenshots where possible (admin dashboard, Telegram order flow, Uzcard checkout). Avoid generic global-commerce stock art.
- **CTA tracking:** route all link-in-bio CTAs to the merchant launch-plan call with a campaign-tagged URL so we can attribute by post ID.
- **Brand review:** all posts go through `brand-review/` before status moves from Draft → In review → Approved.
- **Asset checklist before scheduling:** static layout, Uzbek adaptation, Russian adaptation, alt text, Story frames, CTA link/keyword, UTM, first-hour comment prompt.
- **DM handling:** each keyword needs a prepared response and matching asset before publish. Do not invite `PLAN`, `MARGIN`, `LIVE`, or `TELEGRAM` until the promised asset exists.
- **First-hour engagement:** reply to every comment, pin the clearest CTA comment, and capture useful merchant wording for future VOC.
