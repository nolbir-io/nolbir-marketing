# Instagram — Brick-and-mortar segment batch (Aug 2026)

**Audience:** Uzbekistan merchants who already run a **physical store** (single shop, small chain, bazaar/mall stall, showroom) and know online could grow them — but have held off because "going online" feels like a second full-time job on top of running the shop. Secondary: offline merchants who already take orders informally by phone/Telegram.
**Goal:** Reach the offline-merchant segment, dismantle the "it's too much effort" objection, and drive saves/DMs and launch-plan call bookings.
**Core angle:** *Going online shouldn't become a second job.* Own does the setup and runs the operation underneath — the merchant keeps running the shop they already know.
**Format mix:** 4 carousels, 2 reels. 6 posts — each earns its slot.
**Cadence:** Mon/Wed/Fri. 6 posts spanning 2026-08-03 → 2026-08-14.
**Campaign:** Phase 1 — First 10 (`campaigns/phase-1-brief.md`) — new ICP slice (offline merchants).
**Hashtag stub:** #Own #UzbekistanEcommerce #Tijorat #Dokon #OnlineSavdo #SmallBusinessUz

**Slotting note:** This is a dedicated segment batch. Exact calendar placement against the four-pillar August queue is to be confirmed (`content-creation/social/calendar.md`) — it can run as its own campaign push or absorb segment-relevant four-pillar slots.

**Voice rules:** Practical over hype. Clear over clever. Positive-sum and partner-focused per `brand-voice/voice-guide.md` — the physical store is the heart of the business; online *adds on top*, it doesn't replace it. No fabricated stats or testimonials — every numeric claim is either a structural fact or marked `[PROOF — to collect]`.

**Languages:** Each post carries three versions — **EN (source) · RU · UZ** — transcreated, not translated: each reads natively in its own language. The visual stays the same across all three; only the text layer changes. Native review still required before publish per `brand-voice/language-matrix.md`.

- **CTA tracking:** route all link-in-bio / DM-keyword CTAs to the merchant launch-plan call with a campaign-tagged URL so we can attribute by post ID and by segment (offline-merchant).
- **Localization:** RU/UZ blocks are transcreated, not literal. A native reviewer validates meaning and tone before status moves past In review.
- **Brand review:** all posts go through `brand-review/` before status moves from Draft → In review → Approved.
- **Segment note:** this batch is the first content aimed at offline/physical-store merchants. If it performs, feed the winning hooks back into the persona/audience section of `.agents/product-marketing-context.md`.
