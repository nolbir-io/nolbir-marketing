# Telegram — June 2026 batch (four-pillar launch)

**Audience:** Uzbekistan merchants already selling on Telegram or considering it. Primary: established merchants on Uzum, Yandex Market, Ozon looking for a direct channel.
**Goal:** Establish Own's Telegram presence as a useful, native channel — not a feed mirror. Build engagement through native polls and direct merchant questions.
**Format mix:** Text post, text + native poll.
**Cadence:** 3×/week (Tue/Thu/Sat).
**Campaign:** Phase 1 — First 10 (`campaigns/phase-1-brief.md`)
**Pillar system:** Craft (Apple) · Default (Google) · Movement (Shopify) · Listen — see `docs/strategy/operating-principles.md` and `content-creation/social/calendar.md` ("Four-pillar batch" section).

**Voice rules:** Practical over hype. Clear over clever. No fabricated stats or testimonials — every numeric claim is either a structural fact or marked `[PROOF — to collect]`.

**Languages:** Drafts in English. Telegram reaches Uzbek/Russian-speaking merchants directly — localize to Uzbek (primary) + Russian (secondary) before publish, per `brand-voice/language-matrix.md`.

### TG-2026-06-02 · Text · Draft · Default
Publish: 2026-06-27
Campaign: Phase 1 — First 10

**Hook:** Every order in Uzbekistan has to be reported to the OFD. Most merchants find out the hard way.

**Body:**
- Fiscalization means every sale gets reported to Uzbekistan's tax authority (OFD) in real time.
- Miss it, misconfigure it, or bolt it on late, and you're exposed to compliance risk — not a hypothetical, a requirement.
- Own reports every order at the platform level automatically, across every channel you sell on — no separate vendor, no separate contract.

**Caption:** Compliance shouldn't be the thing you remember after you've already shipped 200 orders.

**CTA:** DM "OFD" if you want the plain-language rundown of what's required and when.
