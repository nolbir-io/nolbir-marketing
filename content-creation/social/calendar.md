## June 2026

### Campaign spine

June is the first public Own narrative batch for **Phase 1 — First 10**. The story should move in this order:

1. **Name the risk:** marketplace dependency is useful, but dangerous when it becomes the whole business.
2. **Make the cost concrete:** take rate, customer access, ranking volatility, and non-compounding ad spend.
3. **Show the alternative:** storefront + Telegram + local payment/delivery rails in one operating system.
4. **Earn trust:** Own measures progress by signed -> live -> transacting, not vanity signups.
5. **Invite action:** DM/comment for a checklist, margin breakdown, or 30-minute launch-plan conversation.
