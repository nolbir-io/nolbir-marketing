# Facebook — June 2026 batch (four-pillar launch)

**Audience:** Uzbekistan SMB merchant owners — slightly older demographic than Instagram, comfortable with longer-form posts and native video.
**Goal:** Reach merchant owners where they already are; mirror Instagram content with adapted tone and length.
**Format mix:** Single image/text post, screenshot post.
**Cadence:** 2×/week (Wed/Sun).
**Campaign:** Phase 1 — First 10 (`campaigns/phase-1-brief.md`)
**Pillar system:** Craft (Apple) · Default (Google) · Movement (Shopify) · Listen — see `docs/strategy/operating-principles.md` and `content-creation/social/calendar.md` ("Four-pillar batch" section).

**Voice rules:** Practical over hype. Clear over clever. No fabricated stats or testimonials — every numeric claim is either a structural fact or marked `[PROOF — to collect]`.

**Languages:** Drafts in English. Localize to Uzbek (primary) + Russian (secondary) before publish, per `brand-voice/language-matrix.md` — Facebook reaches Uzbek/Russian-speaking merchant owners directly.

### FB-2026-06-01 · Single · Draft · Default
Publish: 2026-06-24
Campaign: Phase 1 — First 10

**Hook:** Did you know Telegram can be your actual storefront, not just where you answer customer messages?

**Body:** Many merchants in Uzbekistan already use Telegram to take orders manually — screenshots, manual confirmations, manual payment links. Own turns that into a real sales channel: catalog, checkout, and payment built in, synced with your main storefront so you manage one inventory, not two.

**Caption:** If Telegram is already where your customers find you, it should be where they can actually buy, too.

**CTA:** Comment "TELEGRAM" and we'll walk you through how it works.
