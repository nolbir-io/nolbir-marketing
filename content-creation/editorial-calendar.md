## June 2026 — Own launch narrative

**Campaign:** Phase 1 — First 10
**Primary audience:** established Uzbekistan merchants selling through Uzum, Yandex Market, Ozon, Telegram, or offline retail.
**Secondary audience:** investors, strategic partners, agencies, payment/logistics partners.

### Monthly objective

Use June to make Own's category point simple and repeatable:

> Marketplaces are a useful channel. They should not be the whole business. Own helps merchants build direct commerce infrastructure they control.

### Core assets

| Week | Primary piece | Supporting assets | Owner | Campaign tie-in |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | Marketplace-dependency explainer | IG carousel, IG unit-economics post, LinkedIn thesis post, paid angle A | Marketing | Create urgency for design-partner conversations |
| Week 2 | Customer ownership / hidden cost narrative | IG Reel, IG hidden-cost carousel, LinkedIn "why now" post, Stories poll | Marketing + founder | Turn abstract risk into concrete merchant pain |
| Week 3 | Local rails and activation proof | IG local-rails post, IG signed/live/transacting carousel, LinkedIn team-credibility post | Marketing + product | Show why Own is not a generic global SaaS wrapper |
| Week 4 | Own-your-commerce category close | IG channel Reel, IG multi-channel post, IG definition carousel, LinkedIn category-framing post | Marketing + founder | Move warm audience toward launch-plan calls |

### Missing primary assets to create

These are not yet drafted in the repo, but they would make the social batch stronger:

1. **Merchant launch-plan brief** — the gated/DM asset promised by `PLAN`, `LIVE`, and launch-plan CTAs.
2. **Margin breakdown one-pager** — simple model comparing direct vs marketplace orders, with editable assumptions.
3. **Channel checklist** — storefront, Telegram, payment, delivery, fiscalization, and marketplace-sync readiness.
4. **Founder deck excerpt** — 5-7 public slides for investor/partner DMs, derived from the sales deck.

### Repurposing chain

Each weekly primary piece should generate:

- 1 Instagram carousel or single-image post.
- 1 Reel or Stories sequence.
- 1 LinkedIn founder post or comment thread.
- 1 paid proof angle variation.
- 1 sales enablement snippet for outbound follow-up.

### Measurement

| Signal | What to track | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Saves / shares | Marketplace-risk and checklist posts | Indicates merchant relevance |
| DMs by keyword | `PLAN`, `MARGIN`, `LIVE`, `TELEGRAM` | Shows which pain converts attention |
| Launch-plan call clicks | Link-in-bio and tagged post URLs | Measures conversion intent |
| LinkedIn comments / DMs | Investor and operator posts | Identifies warm partner/investor pipeline |
| Follow growth | Instagram, Telegram, LinkedIn | Validates awareness layer |

## Repurposing Rule

Each long-form piece should generate at least:

- 1 email summary
