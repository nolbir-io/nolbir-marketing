# campaigns

The phased campaign plan that gets Mamurify from where it is today to 1,000 signed merchants.

## Purpose

Hold one brief per campaign — the campaign objective, the phase it serves (first 10, first 100, first 1,000), the channels, the budget, the owner, and the success metrics. Pulls voice from `brand-voice/`, copy from `content-creation/`, and reports back to `performance-analytics/`.

## Key questions to answer

- What are the milestones on the road to 1,000 merchants? (e.g. design partners → early adopters → scaled acquisition)
- What does each milestone require — a different ICP, a different channel mix, a different price point?
- Which campaigns are always-on (e.g. inbound from SEO/content) vs. time-bound pushes (e.g. launch, partner co-marketing)?
- How does merchant acquisition handoff to merchant activation? Where does marketing stop and product/CS begin?
- Budget envelope per phase — what does a CAC ceiling look like?
