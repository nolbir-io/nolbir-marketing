# Phase 1 Campaign Brief - First 10 Design Partners

## Objective

Acquire and activate the first 10 high-fit design-partner merchants.

## ICP Focus

- Digital-first SMB merchants in Uzbekistan with clear operational pain.
- High willingness to collaborate and provide structured feedback.

## Core Message

Own helps merchants launch reliable commerce infrastructure quickly with hands-on onboarding.

## Channels

- Founder-led outbound and warm intros
- Partner referrals (payments, logistics, agency network)
- Targeted educational content

## Budget and Resources

- Low paid media; high founder/relationship effort.
- Resource focus on tailored sales enablement and onboarding assets.

## Success Metrics

- 10 signed design partners
- Signed -> live activation rate above 70%
- Documented qualitative feedback from every partner
