# Voice Guide

Mamurify brand voice standards for external communication.

## Voice Principles

- **Practical over hype:** show operational outcomes, avoid inflated claims.
- **Clear over clever:** short, direct language that merchants can act on.
- **Confident over aggressive:** expert tone without dismissing alternatives.
- **Local relevance:** examples and references should fit Uzbekistan merchant context.

## Do / Do Not

- **Do:** Use concrete merchant pain, measurable outcomes, and clear next steps.
- **Do not:** Use vague superlatives, jargon-heavy claims, or unsupported promises.

## Message Structure

1. Merchant problem in plain language.
2. How Mamurify solves it.
3. Proof or plausible evidence.
4. CTA with one clear next action.

## Terminology Guardrails

- Keep terms consistent across campaigns and reports.
- Maintain shared glossary in campaign briefs where needed.
